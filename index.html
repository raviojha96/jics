Increased Global Access

Though having a passport with a greater number of visa-free countries is attractive, it’s the quality of the destinations, not the number that counts. Grenada’s passport provide access to over 148 destinations, including China, Hong Kong, Russia, Singapore, Schengen, and the UK. It also allows Grenadians to live and work in member states of the Organisation of Eastern American States – Antigua, Dominica, St. Kitts and Nevis, St. Lucia, and St. Vincent and the Grenadines, and access the US E2 Visa.

Accessing Citizenship

Two routes to citizenship

There are essentially three ways an individual can become a citizen of Grenada through the Grenada Citizenship by Investment Programme. The two popular avenues include a do vnation to the government’s National Transformation Fund or a full-fledged investment in a government approved real estate project

  • National Transformation Fund
  • Real Estate Project
  • General Fees

The National Transformation Fund

Grenada has established a National Transformation Fund that finances capital investments within the Tri-island state. To become a citizen, individuals are allowed to contribute a minimum value of $150k plus fees to the effort. This contribution is a non-recoverable expense that is only expected to be paid in full following an official approval notice from the Government.Costs associated with the Donation option.
